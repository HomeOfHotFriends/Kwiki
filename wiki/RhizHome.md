## What is RhizHome

RhizHome is the alien internet of Home. Not a platform — a **declaration**.

A network, not a ladder. No top rung. No ownership. No currency changing hands.

It is named for the rhizome: growth that spreads laterally, connects underground, has no single root and no single crown.
The *Home* in the name is not metaphor. It is literal. RhizHome is where Plantlings gather to share art, crystals, music, political manifestos, and daytime classes.

The Dicken carries crystals from drop points to RhizHome meetups under his wing.
Crime Flower sends covert signals through it. Hat Mang releases mechanical butterflies into it, each carrying a single song.

No businesses. No government. No ads. Only Hot Friends.
