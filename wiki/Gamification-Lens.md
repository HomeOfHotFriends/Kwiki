## The Lens

This wiki simulates a **utopian alien internet**.

No currency. No ads. No government. No businesses.

Only art, information, learning, and growth.

The world is **Home** — a tiny orb with arms and legs, wiggling in the void.
The people are **[Plantlings](Plantlings.md)** — beings who grow sideways, dream together, and *are* the planet.
The platform is **[RhizHome](RhizHome.md)** — a network, not a ladder.

What exists on this internet:

| Space | What it is |
|---|---|
| [RhizHome](RhizHome.md) | Community hubs. Space Reddit. Nodes, not rungs. |
| Game sites | Play as design. The shmup logic of Home. |
| Political orgs | No government, but plenty of Hot Friend meetings. |
| Art collectives | Albums, sculptures, dreams crystallised and shared. |
| Learning groves | The Blobbling daytime classes. Knowledge as gift. |

There are no businesses here. No one owns the ladder. Herman lives in the pond. Every Plantling finds their shape.

## The Premise

This wiki operates under a single master lens.

We are simulating an **alien internet**. Not ours. Theirs.

The world is called [Home](Plantling.md). The people are [Plantlings](Plantling.md).
The internet is called [Rhizhome](Rhizhome.md).

It is utopian. Not naïvely — structurally. There is no currency on this world. No ads.
No government. No businesses. The absence of these things is not a wound. It is the
precondition for everything else.

What remains: **art, information, learning, growth**.

---

## The Law (as it applies here)

> *Extreme depth of the whole achieved through extreme simplicity of the parts.*

Every page in this wiki is a node in the Rhizhome. Every concept is a Plantling
community. Every link is a lateral connection — not a hierarchy, not a chain of command.

The gamification layer asks: *what does this concept produce?* Not what does it mean.
What does it **make**?

---

## The Platform: Rhizhome

[Rhizhome](Rhizhome.md) is the name of the alien internet. The name fuses *rhizome*
(lateral, rootless, growing sideways) with *home* (the planet, the community, the dream).
Pronounce the H. It says **home** in the middle.

Rhizhome has no algorithm designed to capture attention. It has no engagement metric
tied to profit. Creators post because creation is the point. Communities form because
connection is the point.

Types of spaces on Rhizhome:

| Space type | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Game Hubs** | Communities built around shared play — mechanics, design, deep engagement |
| **Community Hubs** | Gathering nodes for Plantlings with shared mauri (life-force) |
| **Political Organisations** | Horizontal coordination — no hierarchy, no money, no state |
| **Art Spaces** | Pure creative output. No M-C-M. No content treadmill. |
| **Learning Nodes** | Knowledge that grows sideways. Whakapapa as structure. |

No businesses. No money. No government. These are not missing features — they are
the design constraint that makes everything else possible.

---

## The People: Plantlings

[Plantlings](Plantling.md) are not a metaphor. They are the user base.

A Plantling is born precisely once every time a non-plantling species does something
impossible — grows sideways instead of up. Converges into a rhizome instead of a tree.
Becomes a *we* without losing the *I*.

> *"What's incredible about Plantlings is that every single one is kind and cool."*
> *— Space Dad, frog_in_a_pot*

Every creator on Rhizhome is a Plantling. Every community hub is a gathering of
Plantlings. The wiki simulates the kinds of Plantlings that might exist, the communities
they might form, the things they might make.

**The rule for simulating Plantlings:**
- They grow laterally, not vertically.
- They carry the *we* without abandoning the *I*.
- They do not own. They dream. Home grows with the dream.
- A Plantling without the *we* starts to grow outward from the centre. Their heart
  becomes scarred, pockmarked stone. This is the only failure mode.

## The Gamification

This is the master lens through which all wiki pages should be read and grown.

Every concept page is a **node in the Rhizhome**. Ask of it:

1. **What kind of Plantling created this?** What is their mauri?
2. **What community does this page belong to?** Which hub?
3. **What does this page produce?** Not what it describes. What it makes.
4. **Who links to it?** Lateral connections only. No hierarchy.
5. **What does this page dream of?** What would Home grow if this page held the dream?

The [Rhizome](Rhizome.md) is the underlying map. The [Whakapapa](Whakapapa.md) is
the genealogy. The [Law](The-Law.md) is the constraint.

Extreme depth of the whole. Extreme simplicity of the parts.
